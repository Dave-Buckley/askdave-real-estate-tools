2

Connect your phone

This lets Ask Dave Real Estate Tools dial numbers through your phone. It's a one-time setup that takes about 5 minutes. Pick your phone and computer combination below.

Android + Windows

Uses Microsoft Phone Link (free, pre-installed on Windows 10/11)
  1. On your PC, search for "Phone Link" in the Start menu and open it
  2. On your Android phone, install "Link to Windows" from the Google Play Store
  3. In Phone Link on your PC, click Get started. It will show a QR code
  4. Open the Link to Windows app on your phone and scan the QR code
  5. Grant all permissions when asked: calls, contacts, messages, notifications
  6. Once paired, go to Phone Link on your PC: Settings → Features → Calls → Turn on
  7. Make a test call. In Phone Link, go to Calls and dial any number to verify it works
Done. When you click "Dial" in Ask Dave Real Estate Tools, the call goes through your Android phone automatically via Phone Link. Your phone rings, you pick up, and you're connected.

Uses Microsoft Phone Link via Bluetooth (free, pre-installed on Windows 10/11)
  1. On your iPhone, make sure Bluetooth is turned on
  2. On your PC, search for "Phone Link" in the Start menu and open it
  3. Choose iPhone when asked which phone to connect
  4. Phone Link will show a QR code. Scan it with your iPhone camera
  5. Tap "Pair" on both your iPhone and PC when prompted
  6. On your iPhone, grant permission for calls, contacts, and notifications
  7. Make a test call from Phone Link to verify it works
Important: This works over Bluetooth, so keep your iPhone within about 10 metres of your PC. Calls go through your iPhone, and you'll hear them through your PC speakers or headset.

3

Set up WhatsApp (optional but recommended)

Most agents use WhatsApp more than regular calls. If you want the "WhatsApp" button to open chats on your computer instead of your phone, install WhatsApp Desktop.

  1. Download WhatsApp Desktop from the Microsoft Store (Windows) or App Store (Mac)
  2. Open it and scan the QR code with your phone's WhatsApp (Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device)
  3. In Ask Dave Real Estate Tools, open Settings and set WhatsApp mode to "Desktop"
That's it. Now when you click "WhatsApp" in Ask Dave Real Estate Tools, it opens the chat directly on your computer in the WhatsApp Desktop app. You can type and send without touching your phone.

Common Questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Ask Dave Real Estate Tools does not record, store, or transmit any calls, messages, or conversations. Your data stays on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.
Do I need to install anything on my phone?
Only if you're on Android + Windows. Install "Link to Windows" from the Play Store. iPhone and WhatsApp users don't need anything extra on their phone.
Can I use WhatsApp without connecting my phone to my computer?
Yes. WhatsApp Desktop works independently once you scan the QR code. You don't need Phone Link for WhatsApp. It just works.
What if I just want WhatsApp for everything and don't need Dial?
Open Ask Dave Real Estate Tools Settings, turn off the Dial feature, and set WhatsApp as your default action. Many agents prefer this setup. You can skip Step 2 entirely.
Does it auto-dial when I click?
No. It opens the number pre-filled and ready. You still press the call button yourself, so you can't accidentally dial someone.
What keyboard shortcuts does Ask Dave Real Estate Tools use?
The default shortcut is Ctrl+Space to detect a phone number from highlighted text. You can also click the tray icon on screen instead. To customise your hotkeys, click the keyboard icon in the Ask Dave Real Estate Tools panel.
Where does Ask Dave Real Estate Tools appear after I install it?
In your system tray (bottom-right of your taskbar on Windows, or the menu bar on Mac). Look for the Ask Dave Real Estate Tools icon. Click it to open the panel, right-click for settings.
Does it work with any CRM?
Yes. Ask Dave Real Estate Tools doesn't connect to your CRM. It works on top of it. If you can see a phone number on your screen, you can highlight it. Works with Bayut, Property Finder, HubSpot, Salesforce, Excel, and anything else.
