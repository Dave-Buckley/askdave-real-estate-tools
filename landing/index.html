2

Connect your phone

This lets AgentKit dial numbers through your phone. It's a one-time setup that takes about 5 minutes. Pick your phone and computer combination below.

Android + Windows

Uses Microsoft Phone Link (free, pre-installed on Windows 10/11)
  1. On your PC, search for "Phone Link" in the Start menu and open it
  2. On your Android phone, install "Link to Windows" from the Google Play Store
  3. In Phone Link on your PC, click Get started. It will show a QR code
  4. Open the Link to Windows app on your phone and scan the QR code
  5. Grant all permissions when asked: calls, contacts, messages, notifications
  6. Once paired, go to Phone Link on your PC: Settings → Features → Calls → Turn on
  7. Make a test call. In Phone Link, go to Calls and dial any number to verify it works
Done. When you click "Dial" in AgentKit, the call goes through your Android phone automatically via Phone Link. Your phone rings, you pick up, and you're connected.

iPhone + Windows BETA

Uses Microsoft Phone Link via Bluetooth (free, pre-installed on Windows 10/11)
  1. On your iPhone, make sure Bluetooth is turned on
  2. On your PC, search for "Phone Link" in the Start menu and open it
  3. Choose iPhone when asked which phone to connect
  4. Phone Link will show a QR code. Scan it with your iPhone camera
  5. Tap "Pair" on both your iPhone and PC when prompted
  6. On your iPhone, grant permission for calls, contacts, and notifications
  7. Make a test call from Phone Link to verify it works
Important: This works over Bluetooth, so keep your iPhone within about 10 metres of your PC. Calls go through your iPhone, and you'll hear them through your PC speakers or headset.

iPhone + Mac BETA

Built-in to Apple. No extra apps needed
  1. Make sure your iPhone and Mac are signed into the same Apple ID
  2. Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network
  3. On your iPhone, go to Settings → Phone → Calls on Other Devices
  4. Turn on "Allow Calls on Other Devices"
  5. In the list below, toggle on your Mac
That's it. When you click "Dial" in AgentKit, your Mac rings and connects through your iPhone automatically. No extra apps, no Bluetooth needed.

Android + Mac BETA

No built-in call handoff, so use WhatsApp instead
  1. There's no built-in way to make phone calls from a Mac through an Android phone
  2. Install WhatsApp Desktop on your Mac from the App Store or whatsapp.com
  3. Open WhatsApp Desktop and scan the QR code with your phone's WhatsApp
  4. In AgentKit settings, set WhatsApp as your default action
Tip: Most agents with this setup use WhatsApp for everything. It works great, and you can call and message directly from your Mac through WhatsApp Desktop.
3

Set up WhatsApp (optional but recommended)

Most agents use WhatsApp more than regular calls. If you want the "WhatsApp" button to open chats on your computer instead of your phone, install WhatsApp Desktop.

  1. Download WhatsApp Desktop from the Microsoft Store (Windows) or App Store (Mac)
  2. Open it and scan the QR code with your phone's WhatsApp (Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device)
  3. In AgentKit, open Settings and set WhatsApp mode to "Desktop"
That's it. Now when you click "WhatsApp" in AgentKit, it opens the chat directly on your computer in the WhatsApp Desktop app. You can type and send without touching your phone.
4

Start using it

AgentKit runs in your system tray. Here's everything you can do.

📞 Highlight to Dial or WhatsApp

See a phone number anywhere on screen? A website, email, CRM, PDF, or spreadsheet? Highlight it with your mouse, then press Shift+Space. A small popup appears with Dial and WhatsApp buttons. Click one.

Shift + Space

🔵 Floating Blue Button

Prefer using the mouse? Click the floating blue circle button on your screen instead of pressing Shift+Space. It does the same thing: reads the highlighted number and shows the action popup.

📝 Send a template message

Click the AgentKit icon in your system tray to open the full panel. Type a contact name, pick a template from the list, and it auto-fills the name. Click "Send via WhatsApp" or copy the message.

⚙ Customise in Settings

Right-click the tray icon and choose Settings. Turn features on or off, switch WhatsApp between phone and desktop mode, and edit your templates. Click the keyboard icon in the panel to customise your hotkeys.

Common Questions

Is my data private?
Yes. AgentKit does not record, store, or transmit any calls, messages, or conversations. Your data stays on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.
Do I need to install anything on my phone?
Only if you're on Android + Windows. Install "Link to Windows" from the Play Store. iPhone and WhatsApp users don't need anything extra on their phone.
Can I use WhatsApp without connecting my phone to my computer?
Yes. WhatsApp Desktop works independently once you scan the QR code. You don't need Phone Link for WhatsApp. It just works.
What if I just want WhatsApp for everything and don't need Dial?
Open AgentKit Settings, turn off the Dial feature, and set WhatsApp as your default action. Many agents prefer this setup. You can skip Step 2 entirely.
Does it auto-dial when I click?
No. It opens the number pre-filled and ready. You still press the call button yourself, so you can't accidentally dial someone.
What keyboard shortcuts does AgentKit use?
The default shortcut is Shift+Space to detect a phone number from highlighted text. You can also click the floating blue button on screen instead. To customise your hotkeys, click the keyboard icon in the AgentKit panel.
Where does AgentKit appear after I install it?
In your system tray (bottom-right of your taskbar on Windows, or the menu bar on Mac). Look for the AgentKit icon. Click it to open the panel, right-click for settings.
Does it work with any CRM?
Yes. AgentKit doesn't connect to your CRM. It works on top of it. If you can see a phone number on your screen, you can highlight it. Works with Bayut, Property Finder, HubSpot, Salesforce, Excel, and anything else.
